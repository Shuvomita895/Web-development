Morisaki Bookshop

Days at the Morisaki Bookshop by Satoshi Yagisawa is a heartwarming Japanese novella about Takako, a young woman who loses her job and boyfriend simultaneously. Seeking refuge, she moves into her eccentric uncle Satoru’s secondhand bookstore in Tokyo’s Jinbōchō book district. Through reading, connecting with eccentric customers, and helping her uncle, she finds healing and a new path forward.
