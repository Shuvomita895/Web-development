FAQ

Common questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out to our support team and we'll get back to you within a few hours.

Is there really a free plan?

Yes. The Starter plan is free forever with up to 5 members and 10 projects. No credit card required to sign up.

Can I import from other tools?

Absolutely. We support direct import from Trello, Jira, Asana, Monday.com, and Notion. Most imports complete in under a minute.

What happens when my trial ends?

You'll be moved to the free Starter plan automatically. No charges, no data loss. You can upgrade anytime to keep your Pro features.

Is my data secure?

We're SOC 2 Type II certified, use AES-256 encryption at rest, and TLS 1.3 in transit. Enterprise plans also get SSO, audit logs, and custom data residency options.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your billing page with one click. You'll keep access until the end of your current billing period. No cancellation fees.
